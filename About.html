About Nkoni

Nkoni Properties is a South African based real estate development and management company focusing on residential, retail, commercial and hospitality properties. The company was formed in the year 2021 with the aim of transforming and changing lives and restoring dignity of the previously disadvantaged communities by developing rural neighborhood spaces into modern precinct underpinned by residential apartments.

Over the years we have grown and expanded our business to retail and commercial interests thereby offering our clients a wide range of services. Our rural upbringing and living experience has reinforced our passion to develop retail and residential assets that change the landscape of the areas which in turn helps develop such nodes into sustainable living communities at an economic, environmental, and social level. Our property portfolio is predominantly located in Limpopo and Gauteng Province, but we aim to have a national footprint by 2025. The formation of this company is attributed to the zeal and commitment of our founding member in ensuring quality economic and residential developments to the disadvantaged communities and improving their lives through access to quality and affordable residential, retail and commercial developments.

Why Us?

We take cognizance of the fact that the environment and communities within which we operate are the cornerstone of our existence and our company values are embodied with such principles. We strive to continuously hold high standards of environmental, social and governance in our business operations. We have a large, competent and multi-skilled workforce that allows us to undertake developments projects internally from inception to completion. Our associated companies under the umbrella of Nkoni Investments, comprised of Consulting Civil, Electrical and Mechanical engineering, Architectural work and Quantity Surveying. These resources consists of professional registered civil, electrical engineers and technologists as well as Architects, Quantity surveyors and Project Managers with vast experience in tendering, procurement alternative designs and construction management..
