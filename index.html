About us

Nkoni Properties is a South African based real estate development and management company focusing on residential, retail, commercial and hospitality properties. The company was formed in the year 2021 with the aim of transforming and changing lives and restoring dignity of the previously disadvantaged communities by developing rural neighborhood spaces into modern precinct underpinned by residential apartments. Over the years we have grown and expanded our business to retail and commercial interests thereby offering our clients a wide range of services.
